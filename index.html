About

Lush vocals, soulful harmonies and crisp beats come together in the music of Brooklyn-based producer Josh Jacobson. Josh leverages his lifelong background as a jazz pianist with a distinctively song-oriented approach to craft an organic, harmonically rich style of electronic music.

Josh has been featured on blogs like Nest HQ, Run the Trap, Suicide Sheep, and Sensible Reason, and has gotten airplay on Rinse FM.
